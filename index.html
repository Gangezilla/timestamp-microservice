Timestamp Service

Hi there, this is a little timestamp converter tool that served as practice for me with express, and coding more outside of work. If you type in a date into the URL above in the format of '19.01.2011', '31 January 1992', or a Unix Timestamp such as '495923023' you'll have this returned to you. Yeah, it is pretty cool.

Currently, this tool will accept spoken dates in the format 'date month year', or '31 January 1992', natural dates as '31.01.1992' and unix timestamps.
